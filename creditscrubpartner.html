05 — The Terms

How This Stays Fair

Generous commission on the front end, structural protections on the back end. Both sides have skin in the game.

01 MRR Commission Sunset

Full commission (40% or 50%) on monthly recurring revenue for the first 24 months. Months 25–36 drops to half. Months 37+ settles at 10% perpetual. Your deals keep paying long after you stop thinking about them.

02 90-Day Activation

First qualifying intro within 90 days of signing. No production by day 90 → partnership moves to Inactive status. Not terminated — paused. Reactivates automatically when your next intro lands.

03 90-Day Quality Protection

If a client cancels within 90 days of signing, 50% of setup commission claws back and MRR commission stops. Protects both sides — no incentive to push bad-fit leads, no risk of a quick-cancel draining your pocket.

04 1099 Independent Contractor

No employment. No exclusivity. No non-compete. You run your businesses, we run ours, the partnership lives alongside both. Cancel anytime, no drama.
