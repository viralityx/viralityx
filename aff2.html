Ideas to make it bigger

If you want to get creative

Bring a local owner on and run a live scan

Have a guest, a shop owner, a contractor, whoever, and we run the free scan on their business live. You read the results on air. It's real content, it's useful, and every listener who owns a business will want theirs.

Do a "what's actually killing small businesses" episode

Missed calls, no follow-up, no systems. Real talk about why good people with good businesses stay stuck. We'll give you the data. You keep the episode.

Follow Nick's story over time

Check in every month or two on what changed for him. That's a built-in case study your audience is already invested in.

When people DM you

Don't answer, just route

You're going to get questions. "What does it cost?" "Would it work for my business?" You don't have to know any of it.

Copy-paste reply

"Honestly no idea what it'd cost for you, it depends on what they find. Best thing is the free scan, it just shows you where you're leaking. Here's the link: [your link] , tell them I sent you."

Rules

Four things to never do

  • Never quote a price. Every business is different. "Depends what they find" is always the right answer.
  • Never promise results. Don't say it'll double anybody's business. Share your experience, that's it.
  • Never skip the disclosure. One sentence, every read. Non-negotiable.
  • Never send someone you wouldn't vouch for. Your name is on this too.
