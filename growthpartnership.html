04 — Revenue Share Model

Aligned incentives.
We win when you win.

The performance model is simple. We invest in building and running the investor funnel. You close the deals. We earn when capital moves.

How It Works

This isn't a retainer where you pay whether results come or not. Our earnings are directly tied to funded deals generated by our system.

  • We build the investor acquisition funnel with a shared investment to get it live
  • Every qualified investor lead is delivered to Anthony for a personal conversation
  • When a lead converts into a funded deal, a pre-agreed referral fee is paid to ViralityX
  • No funded deal = no ongoing fee. Skin in the game from both sides
  • Exact fee structure, payment timing, and terms will be defined together on the strategy call
  • This model scales with your deal volume — more deals, more revenue for both sides

Why this works for Amplified: Your ongoing cost is tied to results, not a flat monthly retainer. Every referral fee you pay is connected to an investor who funded a deal. Your cost of acquisition is predictable and performance-based.


Why this works for ViralityX: We're building a system that generates recurring revenue as long as Amplified keeps closing deals — which, at 150+ transactions per year, is not a question. Both sides invest upfront because both sides believe in the outcome.
