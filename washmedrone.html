What The Audit Found

Where revenue is leaking right now.

Full audit completed on washmedrone.com — every page, copy, schema, tracking, and conversion path. Six findings are blocking the business from closing the deals it's already capable of running.

Finding 01 · Measurement

Zero analytics installed. No GA4, no Meta Pixel, no call tracking, no session replay. Every visitor and every phone call is invisible. Marketing spend is unattributable.

Finding 02 · Positioning

NDAA / Apellix Blue is buried. The single most valuable differentiator in the category appears only on the About page, never in the homepage hero. Federal, state, and defense-prime buyers can't find it.

Finding 03 · Social Proof

Gallery is empty. Five total images, zero before/afters. The site claims "500+ satisfied customers" with no review widget, no testimonials, no logo wall.

Finding 04 · Trust Signals

Contact email is a gmail address. washmedrone@gmail.com on a site pitching $50K commercial contracts. License numbers (Part 107, CSLB) are claimed but not shown.

Finding 05 · Search & Schema

SEO foundation is broken. Title tag says "Drone Window Cleaning" but service list is far broader. No LocalBusiness schema. No service-area pages. A staging "/test/" page is live and indexed.

Finding 06 · Lead Capture

Quote form is a barebones generic intake. No property type, no square footage, no service qualifier. Every lead lands in a flat queue with no automation, nurture, or follow-up.

The Wedges

Four advantages no California competitor has.

The audit doubled as a competitive scan. We mapped every active drone-cleaning operator in California — California Drone Cleaning, AltitudeWash, Maxx ECO Wash, LA Drone Wash, DroneWash+, Ray Access Pro, Sky Wash Drones, Drone Cleaning Company, and Droneworx. The market is fragmented. No dominant player. Four wedges are open and WashMeDrone can take all of them.

01. NDAA / Blue UAS / DoD-Cleared

WashMeDrone's Apellix Blue is the only NDAA-compliant exterior cleaning drone certified for U.S. Department of Defense use. Zero competitors are publicly leveraging this. Opens federal, state, military (Pendleton, Miramar, Edwards, Vandenberg, March ARB), defense primes (Lockheed, Northrop, Raytheon, Boeing, Anduril), UC/CSU campuses, and critical infrastructure (ports, airports).

02. Sister-brand referral engine (PPHI)

Every Pacific Property Home Inspections inspection touches a roof, gutters, and exterior — generating qualified, photo-proofed wash leads at near-zero CAC. No drone-wash competitor has this loop. Expected: 8–15% of inspections become wash jobs.

03. 4-county SoCal coverage under one brand

LA, OC, SD, and Riverside under one vendor agreement. Regional property management firms (CBRE, JLL, Cushman, Greystar) prefer single-vendor coverage across metros. Every active competitor we surveyed is LA-only or Bay-only.

04. Productized residential packages

Every competitor surveyed is "contact for quote." WashMeDrone publishes tiered, fixed-price residential packages tied to an instant-quote calculator — winning the volume / paid-search game while competitors are still emailing PDFs.
