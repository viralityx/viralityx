Section 01

Purovitalis is a premium European longevity supplement brand. They sell NMN, NAD+ boosters, resveratrol, spermidine, quercetin, GlyNAC, berberine, collagen, and a handful of other longevity SKUs. 4.6 stars on Trustpilot with 412+ reviews. Real brand, real product, real customer base. Not a startup, not a dropshipper.

Section 02

The strategic angle you lead with

Acquisition cost in supplement DTC has roughly doubled since 2022. The brands winning today are not winning on cheaper CAC, they are winning on retention. Top-quartile longevity brands have moved from 36% to 55%+ annual retention through three things: structured save-flows, AI customer service, and a clinical authority signal that justifies premium pricing.

Frame it founder to founder: "You built a premium European brand with a moat (liposomal + clean-label + Aura biomarker app). The question for the next 36 months is not how to acquire more customers, it's how to keep the ones you have for 18 months instead of 4. That's where ViralityX comes in. We don't run your ads. We rebuild the operations layer that determines whether a subscriber sticks for 4 months or 18."

Section 05

Industry-specific hooks (only mention if natural)

  • Aura app is wasted potential until it's wired in. Right now it looks like a marketing-acquisition app. The 50+ biomarkers should be feeding the subscription engine: positive cardiovascular movement triggers a heart-health SKU offer, stagnated energy biomarkers trigger an NAD+ Booster offer, missed scans trigger re-engagement. None of that is visibly wired today.
  • Clinical authority is thin relative to price. Koen's founder story is strong but does not by itself carry the clinical weight a premium NAD+ stack needs. Tru Niagen has ChromaDex. Timeline has Amazentis SA. Purovitalis charges premium prices but doesn't yet show named PhD or MD advisors. This is a structural vulnerability as the category professionalizes.
  • Safety-question routing is a regulatory landmine. "I'm on metformin, can I take berberine" is a question that needs a guarded routing layer, not a generic AI answer. One wrong response is bigger than the labor savings of automation. We build the guardrails into the AI agent before it goes live.
  • Trustpilot volume gap. 412 reviews vs Tru Niagen's 10K+ and Timeline's 5K+. The 3-month Start Plan framing is perfect for a triggered review request at day 90 (the moment the customer feels the effect). Cheapest path to closing that gap.
  • Multi-region stack. EU site + US site + Mongolia retail + EU distribution partners. Likely separate backends, separate customer identities, separate support routing. Multi-region brands lose 5 to 12% of revenue to this fragmentation.
