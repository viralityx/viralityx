ViralityX  ×  Diamond Edge Construction

Growth Systems Services Agreement

Effective date: ______________, 2026
This Agreement is entered into between ViralityX ("ViralityX," "we," "us"), and Diamond Edge Construction, LLC, represented by Robert Jackson ("Client," "you"). The parties agree as follows.

1. What we do

ViralityX will build, operate, and manage growth and operations systems for the Client, including lead capture and follow-up, government opportunity monitoring, a sales pipeline and tracking, subcontractor intake, and reputation and review systems, plus ongoing management and optimization of those systems.

2. Fees

Operating retainer. $500 per month, billed monthly, covering the operation and management of the systems.
No setup fee. The standard build and setup fee is waived for the initial term as an investment in the partnership.
Performance fee. 15% of the Gross Profit on any job Sourced by ViralityX (defined in Section 3), payable as the Client collects payment on that job.
Example. On a $200,000 job Sourced by ViralityX at a 35% gross margin ($70,000 gross profit), the performance fee is $10,500. The Client retains the remainder.
90-day review. At 90 days from the effective date, the parties will review results and may agree in writing to a revised structure, such as a set retainer or annual fee plus a continuing performance share.

3. What counts as "Sourced by ViralityX"

A job is "Sourced by ViralityX" if the lead or opportunity was generated or captured through the systems, channels, or outreach that ViralityX builds or operates, including the website capture, follow-up, advertising, search presence, the government opportunity monitor, and direct ViralityX referrals. Government contracts awarded through the monitoring system count as Sourced.
The Client's pre-existing referral network and any clients the Client brings independently are not Sourced by ViralityX, and no performance fee applies to them. Ever.
ViralityX maintains the pipeline and tracking system that records the source of each lead. That record governs attribution for performance fees.

4. Ownership

The Client owns its own data, contacts, brand, and content. ViralityX retains all ownership of its systems, tools, methods, workflows, templates, and intellectual property, including anything developed to deliver the services. This Agreement does not transfer ViralityX's systems or methods to the Client.

5. Term and termination

This Agreement begins on the effective date and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months, then month to month. Either party may terminate with thirty (30) days written notice.
Performance-fee tail. If this Agreement ends, the performance fee still applies to any job Sourced by ViralityX before termination, or in the pipeline at termination, that the Client is awarded or closes within one hundred eighty (180) days after termination.
On termination, ViralityX may deactivate its systems. The Client keeps its own data and contacts.

6. Working relationship

The parties are independent contractors. Nothing here creates an employment relationship, partnership, or joint venture, or authorizes either party to bind the other. Each party keeps the other's non-public business information confidential.

7. The spirit of this deal

We are structuring this lean and betting on the partnership because we believe in what you are building. Our real upside only shows up when you win work we created. We win when you win.
