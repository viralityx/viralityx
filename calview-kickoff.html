What We'll Cover at the Kickoff.

Suggested 60-minute meeting agenda. We don't have to march through it in order — this is a checklist, not a script. Whatever doesn't get covered live, I'll follow up over text.

0–5 min
Confirm the deal in writing

5–15 min
Walk through the access checklist together

Knock out the easy ones live — Google Ads, GBP, TintWiz, Wix. If you can grant access from your phone during the call, we're already ahead.

15–35 min
The "ideal customer" deep-dive

This is the most valuable part of the meeting. We talk through your last 3 dream jobs, the customers you wish you could clone, the jobs you'd love to never touch again. Everything we build flows from this.

35–45 min
Service area + budget sanity check

Map your "yes zones" and confirm a realistic monthly ad budget. I'll show you what comparable OC shops are spending so you have context.

45–55 min
Define the 90-day goal

Pick the one metric we judge success by. Closed jobs? Revenue? Booked-out weeks? Whatever it is, we both write it down so there's no ambiguity in 90 days.

55–60 min
Next steps + timeline

I lay out the build order, when campaigns go live, and what you'll see in week 1, week 2, and week 4. You leave knowing exactly what's happening and when.

— Part 03

What Happens After.

Rough build timeline. Things shift based on how fast access lands and how clean the existing assets are, but this is the shape of the first 30 days.

Week 1
Foundation week

Access granted, accounts audited, conversion tracking installed, TintWiz wired for offline conversions, smart web form built and tested on Wix, baseline analytics captured so we know what "before" looks like.

Week 2
Build week

Campaign architecture (residential, commercial, security split), keyword research finalized, negative keywords loaded, ad copy written, first landing page live, geo-targeting locked.

Week 3
Launch week

Campaigns go live with conservative bids. First leads start flowing. We watch like hawks, daily check-ins via text on what's working. Quick adjustments — don't expect smooth performance yet, this is the calibration phase.

Week 4+
Optimization mode

By now we have real data — which keywords drove leads, which leads closed, which ad copy converted. We start the cycle of test → ship winners → kill losers. From here on, the system gets sharper every week.

Day 60–90
First real read

Enough closed-job data has flowed back through TintWiz that Google's algorithm starts optimizing for booked jobs, not just form fills. Cost-per-closed-customer becomes a real, trackable number. This is when ROI clarity arrives.
