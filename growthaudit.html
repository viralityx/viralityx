01 — What We Found

Your audit results.

We reviewed coconutgrovedental.com, your scheduling system, your Google presence, and how your patient acquisition pipeline compares to top dental practices in the Miami market. There are significant gaps costing you new patients every week.

1

No automated lead follow-up

When a potential patient visits your website and doesn't book, they're gone. No SMS follow-up, no email sequence, no retargeting. Every visitor who leaves without booking is a lost patient.

2

FlexBook scheduling with no automation layer

You're using FlexBook for online booking, but there are no automated confirmations via SMS, no reminder sequences to reduce no-shows, and no post-visit follow-up to drive reviews or rebookings.

3

No 24/7 patient engagement

Your office hours are Monday through Thursday. Anyone searching for a dentist Friday through Sunday gets no response from your practice. No chatbot, no after-hours booking, no instant reply.

4

Strong design but weak conversion architecture

The site looks professional and the branding is solid. But there's no lead capture on service pages, no exit-intent offers, no chat widget, and limited calls to action beyond the single booking link.

5

SEO and local search gaps

Service pages for veneers, implants, and general dentistry exist but lack the depth and keyword optimization needed to rank against Miami dental practices investing heavily in SEO.

6

No automated review generation

Patient testimonials on the website are a great start. But there's no system automatically requesting Google reviews after appointments. In dental, reviews are the number one factor driving new patient calls.

03 — The Solution

What we would build
for Coconut Grove Dental.

A complete patient acquisition and retention system that runs alongside your existing website and scheduling. No disruption to your current operations — we layer automation on top of what already works.

Phase 01

Capture and Respond

Weeks 1-2
