03 — What's Holding You Back

A Premium Brand Trapped Behind Manual Walls.

Here's what we identified after auditing your site, your current lead flow, and the industry-standard issues every solo tinter faces.

⏱️

Solo Installer = Slow Lead Response

You're the installer AND the salesperson. When you're 20 minutes into a rear window install with hands wet, every lead has to wait. In tinting, lead response time is the #1 conversion factor — slow responses mean customers call the next shop on the list.

📋

Generic Wix Contact Form

Your current website lead capture is a basic name/email/dropdown form. No instant alert to your phone, no qualification, no automated follow-up. Submissions sit in your inbox until you have time — by then the lead is cold or already booked elsewhere.

💬

No Quote Follow-Up System

Tinting customers shop around. They get 3–5 quotes before deciding. If you send yours and never follow up, you lose to whoever does. Right now there's no system handling that — and every unfollowed quote is a lead going to a competitor.

⭐

No Review Engine

You're a new brand competing against shops with 20+ years of accumulated reviews. Without an automated system asking every happy customer for one, you'll never close that gap. Reviews are the #1 trust signal in local services.

🔍

No Google Ads or Conversion Tracking

You're paying for ads on Yelp and Thumbtack but with no visibility into which leads actually convert into paying jobs. No conversion tracking, no keyword data, no way to know what's working and what's wasting budget.

🎖️

Veteran-Owned Story Underleveraged

Your veteran-owned status is one of the strongest trust signals you have — and it's barely visible across your funnel. This is a brand differentiator your competitors literally cannot copy. We need to make it the centerpiece of every touchpoint.
