Section 01

What they actually do (in plain language)

You know this better than the audit document does, so this section is for the muscle memory of how you describe it to Daniel before the call. Marketwise is a boutique Appraisal Management Company. They sit between mortgage lenders (banks, credit unions, mortgage bankers) and licensed appraisers. The lender wants a property valued for a loan. Marketwise routes the order to a qualified appraiser on the panel, manages the report through QC, ensures USPAP and Dodd-Frank compliance, submits to UCDP, and delivers the final report back to the lender. The fee is a per-order split. The product is residential plus commercial, with AVM and Property Condition Report add-ons.

The business won the Inc. 5000 in 2023 at rank 2629. Marcus founded it in 2017 with a deliberate boutique thesis: stay small enough to actually pick up the phone, but cover every state so national lenders don't have to use a second AMC for out-of-footprint files. The bet has worked. The next 24 months are the test of whether the operational backbone scales past founder-led execution. That tension is the entire pitch.
