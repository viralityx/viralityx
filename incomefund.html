Section 07

Objection prep (rehearse these once)

"My fund admin already handles the LP communications."

You: Right, and we don't replace the fund admin. Fund admins handle statements, K-1s, and distribution mechanics, that's their lane. What they don't do is the pre-wire nurture sequence between opt-in and commitment, which is where 80% of accredited leads die. They also don't build the LP portal experience that drives 12-month renewals and referrals. Different layer, different stage of the LP lifecycle.

"I have an agency running the investwithmcc.com paid traffic already."

You: Good, traffic is solved then. The audit isn't about traffic, it's about what happens AFTER the click. Your traffic agency drives the opt-in. We build the 90-day automated sequence that converts that opt-in into a wired commitment. Traffic without nurture is just expensive lead generation. We're the layer that turns the leads they generate into AUM.

"Accredited investors are sophisticated. They don't need automation, they need a real conversation."

You: 100% agree they need a real conversation. The question is what's in the 60 to 180 day window BEFORE that conversation gets booked. Accredited investors are sophisticated, which means they're ALSO comparing your follow-up cadence against iCapital and Yieldi. They don't articulate "your nurture sequence felt thin," they just go cold. Automation isn't replacing the call, it's making sure the call actually happens.

"I don't want to ask my LPs for video testimonials."

You: Totally fair concern, that's a real ask. Two angles. One, you don't need video for all of them, even three real named quotes with photos and titles would 2x the page's conversion. Two, the LPs who write the biggest checks usually WANT to talk about why they invested. They're proud of the placement. The ask is lower friction than it feels. We help you script the request.

"How much is this going to cost?"

You: Depends on which gaps you want to close first. The borrower-side and investor-side builds are different builds with different scopes. That's why the Blueprint Review with Daniel is the right next step. He'll walk you through what we'd build, in what order, and you'll get a real number against your actual situation. Pricing without context just sounds like a number you'd argue with.
