The system, part by part
01

Lead Capture

What it does

Every inquiry, from your website, your phone, and referrals, lands in one place the moment it happens. No lead sits in a form or a voicemail you find later.

In practice

Someone reaches out. Within seconds, they are captured and logged, and you can see them. It works the four days your office is quiet and after hours, the same as during the day.

What you get

Not a single interested person slips away because no one was at the desk.

02

Instant Response & Follow-Up

What it does

The moment a lead comes in, they get an immediate, professional response, and then a paced follow-up cadence until they book a conversation or tell us they are not ready.

In practice

First touch within minutes. Then a structured sequence of follow-ups over the following days, by text and email, in your voice, so you are the first credible builder to respond and the one who stays top of mind.

What you get

You beat competitors on speed without lifting a finger, and warm leads stop going cold.

03

Government Opportunity Monitoring

What it does

Your system watches the federal and state contracting sources every day and surfaces only the set-aside opportunities that fit your size, your trade, and your region, with their deadlines.

In practice

Instead of oversized jobs you cannot bond, you get a short, relevant list of right-sized opportunities you are actually positioned to win as a service-disabled veteran-owned builder, delivered to you on a regular cadence.

What you get

The certification you already hold finally works as a real, steady pipeline.

04

Pipeline & Tracking

What it does

Every opportunity, bid, and job moves through clear stages, so you always know where each pursuit stands and what the next step is. We track your win rates as the data builds.

In practice

Nothing lives in your head or a spreadsheet anymore. You open one view and see the whole board, and over time we can see which kinds of jobs you win so we point your energy there.

What you get

Control of the pipeline, and real historical data for the first time.

05

Subcontractor Intake

What it does

Subs who reach out are captured, acknowledged right away, and organized into a ready bench with the details you need.

In practice

Instead of a form that goes nowhere, every applicant is logged and sorted, so when a job lands you already have a labor pool to pull from.

What you get

A bench built before you need it, not scrambled together mid-job.

06

Reputation

What it does

After a job wraps, a request goes out to collect a review, and your rating gets surfaced where prospective clients and referral partners look.

In practice

Your strong reviews stop being invisible and start showing up first when someone checks you out before choosing a builder.

What you get

In a market where they do not know you yet, you get found and trusted faster.
