Referral Partner Agreement

Effective date: ______________, 2026
This agreement is between ViralityX ("ViralityX," "we," "us") and __________________________ ("you," "Referral Partner"). It's short on purpose. Here's exactly how this works.

1. What you do

You introduce business owners who might benefit from what we do, using your unique referral link or by sending us their name and contact directly. That's the whole job. You don't sell, quote prices, or make promises on our behalf.

2. What you earn

$500 for every referral that closes, meaning a business you referred signs with us and makes their first payment.

Larger projects may pay more than $500. If a referral turns into something bigger, we'll agree on that number with you in writing before it closes. We'd rather pay you more than lose you.

3. When you get paid

Within 15 days of the referred client's first payment clearing. Paid however you prefer, PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, or check.

4. What counts as your referral

  • They came through your unique link, or
  • You sent us their name and contact before we'd already been in touch with them, and we confirmed it.

Our system records the source of every lead. That record decides it. If there's no record, there's no fee, so always use your link or text us the intro.

5. What doesn't count

  • Yourself or a business you own.
  • Anyone already in our pipeline, or a current or former client, before you referred them.
  • Duplicates. If two partners refer the same business, the first one on record gets paid.
  • Anyone who doesn't sign and pay.

6. Taxes

If you earn $600 or more in a calendar year, we're required by law to report it and issue you a 1099. So we'll need a W-9 from you before your first payout. We'll send it over, it takes two minutes.

7. Honest promotion

Share your real experience all day. But please don't guarantee results, quote prices, or speak on ViralityX's behalf. Every deal is custom, so pricing conversations are ours to have.

If you promote us to an audience (a podcast, social media, a newsletter, a group), you must clearly say that you're a paid partner and earn money if someone signs up. It's an FTC requirement and it protects both of us. One sentence does it.

8. Ending this

Either of us can end this agreement at any time, just let the other know in writing. Any referral you made before it ends still earns you the fee if it closes within 90 days after.

9. The relationship

You're an independent referral partner, not an employee, agent, or partner of ViralityX, and you can't sign or commit to anything on our behalf. We each keep the other's private business information private.

10. The spirit of it

We only want referrals you'd actually stand behind. Send us people you like, we'll take care of them, and you get paid. If we ever make you look bad, tell us and we'll fix it.
