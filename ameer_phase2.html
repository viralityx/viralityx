Design Notes

Tracking URL: coral-med.com/checkup — tags leads as "source: reactivation-mailer" in CRM.
Emotional, not clinical. "It's been a while" feels personal, not like a mass mailing. The quote on the back reinforces the personal touch.
Physical + digital combo: This mailer goes out to patients identified by the 90-day reactivation workflow. Patients who don't respond to the text get the physical card as a follow-up. Double-touch.
Mailing list source: Pull from patient records — anyone with a last visit date 6+ months ago. Requires address data from Office Ally.

Design Notes

Tracking URL: coral-med.com/wellness — tags leads as "source: wellness-spotlight" in CRM.
Why wellness first: It's the most universally relevant service, covered by most insurance, and gives Ameer the 30-min new patient slot he wants. It's also the gateway visit — once they're in for a wellness exam, they become a regular patient.
Future spotlight cards: Same template, swap the service. Weight loss, aesthetics, chronic disease management, sick visits, telehealth. Test which services drive the most scans/calls and double down.
"Covered by insurance" is the hook. Removes the cost objection immediately. Most people don't know their annual physical is free.
